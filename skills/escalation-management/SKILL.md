# Escalation Management

## Escalation packet

Include:

- Customer/account context
- Impact and severity
- Timeline and SLA deadline
- Reproduction steps or evidence
- What support already tried
- Requested owner/team
- Customer-facing update draft

## Rule

Escalations must be specific enough that the receiving team can act without asking support to re-triage.
